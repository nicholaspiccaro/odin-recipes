Onionade

Onionade is a delectable treat, full of the fruits of this worlds labor. It involves a striking tartness balanced by a full sweetness that just brings tears to your eyes. You CANNOT skip this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Cheesecloth
  • Water(5 cups)
  • Sugar(1 cups)
  • 4 onions(or 1 cup Onion juice)

Steps

  1. Peel and chop onions, then blend them, then strain them through cheesecloth into container. This should yield roughly 1 cup of onion juice.
  2. Make simple syrup. Mix one cup of water and one cup of sugar into saucepan and heat until all sugar is dissolved.
  3. Mix simple syrup into onion juice then finally add 4 cups of water or ice and refrigerate.
  4. Enjoy! Perfect for birthday parties!
