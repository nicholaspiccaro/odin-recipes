Milk Pasta

This wonderful recipe will remind you of childhood, coming home from a long day at school to a nice fresh bowl of milk pasta. This cold snack is easy to whip up and will leave you refreshed and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • Boxed Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Whole Milk(3 cups)

Steps

  1. First start boiling the water, and salt the water, once boiling add pasta and cook for around 12 minutes or until pasta is al-dente.
  2. Once pasta is cooked, strain and put into a bowl.
  3. Pour cold milk over cooked pasta.
  4. Enjoy! Perfect for hungry kiddos!
