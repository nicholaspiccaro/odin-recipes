Pickled Snickers

Craving something sweet, salty, and savory? Oh boy do I have the recipe for you. This recipe is a creative twist on pigs in a blanket. It will have all the kid's parents jealous of how much of a cool mom you are.

Ingredients

  • Pickle Large
  • Snickers

Steps

  1. Cut your pickle in half, and begin by hollowing out your large pickle.
  2. Once hollowed, insert your Snickers bar into the hollowed pickle and seal up the pickle
  3. This pickley desserty treat is ready for the hungry kiddos.
